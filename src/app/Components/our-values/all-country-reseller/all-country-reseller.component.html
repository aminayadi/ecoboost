Benefits

EcoBoost™ is a one-stop solution with many benefits.
It is an advanced enzyme-based solution for increasing fuel efficiency and engine reliability.

Deep clean

In storage, the interaction with old and stale fuel and any contaminants present, accelerate calorific degradation and flocculation. This affects combustion efficiency and increases emission levels from the commingled fuel creating carbon and soot build-up in engines and exhaust.

EcoBoost™ enzymes are powerful natural surfactants and dispersants. Fuel sludge is bio-remediated and harmful contaminants such as bacteria, fungi, and mold are kept in check. As a result, fuel filters remain cleaner, separator workload is reduced and the Stoichiometric ratio restored.

EcoBoost™ aids in the maintenance of fuel tank and fuel delivery systems, eliminate carbon build-ups in engines and exhausts.

Performance & savings

A clean engine shows better performance than one obstructed with carbon build-ups, damaged pumps, worn injectors, etc. This improved performance is marked by the reduction of lubricant oil consumption.

On one hand, said lube oil is clearer and cleaner than before using the EcoBoost™ enzyme fuel treatment.

On the other hand, fuel that burns better, quicker and more completely induces better efficiency, i.e. more power for the same consumption or reduced fuel consumption for the same power output.

EcoBoost™ fuel additive eliminates the fuel over-consumption caused by contaminants in the tanks and carbon and soot build-ups in the engines.

Environmental health

EcoBoost™ is dedicated to protecting and preserving the environment: first of all, by its entirely organic base composition, the EcoBoost™ Enzyme Fuel Technology cannot harm the environment. This is part of a more globally sustainable development scheme.

The environmental impact from using petroleum-derived fuels and the effect on human and animal health are becoming clear. EcoBoost treated fuels can lower

the harmful environmental impact. Recently discovered renewable fuels (biofuels etc.) have also shown to benefit from the addition of EcoBoost enzyme technology when used in boilers.
